The DigiCard Process

Since DigiCard is a universal credit card, it can be bought at any bank or here online for a one-time price. To apply for DigiCard, you have to register for an account and then choose your DigiCard plan and your method of payment. After that, you can enjoy shopping with your brand new DigiCard without paying any monthly fees.

DigiCard Requirements

To get a DigiCard, you need to have at least one current bank account with any bank and a good credit score would be recommended. You also need to register for a DigiCard account and give your current bank account information. To apply for a higher plan than a regular DigiCard, you need to have a good credit score.

DigiCard Plans

Basic DigiCard

With the Basic DigiCard, you only pay $50 for compatibility with hundreds of banks around the world and one hundred installed apps in the DigiCard. The Basic DigiCard arrives in the mail in five to seven business days.

Premium DigiCard

With the Premium DigiCard, you pay $70 for compatibility with a thousand banks around the world and hundreds of pre-installed apps in the DigiCard. The Premium DigiCard arrives in the mail in two to three business days.

Super DigiCard

With the Super DigiCard, you pay $95 for compatibility with thousands of banks around the world and a thousand pre-installed apps in the DigiCard. The Super DigiCard arrives in the mail in the next business day.
