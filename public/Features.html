DigiCard Features

Physical Features

DigiCard is made out of glass so it can be touch screen like any other smart device. It is basically treated like any other credit card, except that it is the “mother of all credit cards.” Don't worry! Just because DigiCard is a smart device doesn't mean that it cannot fit into your wallet.

ATM Use

DigiCard can be used on any ATM or bank without paying extra fees for taking out money from a bank or ATM that does not match the card. This saves you a lot of time and money by not having to go to a certain bank or ATM. Who knows? You can even go to the bank right by your house.

Bank Compatibility

Since it is a universal credit card, DigiCard can be used with any bank you choose, from Chase to Bank of America to TD Bank to several international banks. You name it! In addition, you do not have to pay extra fees if you use your DigiCard with a bank that you're not part of.


Digital Features

DigiCard can also be used in the digital realm in which you can check the information of all of your credit cards (such as balance, account, and payments) with one simple press of a button. Therefore, DigiCard can be treated like a phone or a tablet, except that it is a credit card.

Digital Interface

With one simple press of a button, you can access a modern digital menu which gives you a few options, from surfing the web to managing payments and bills all the way to using smartphone-like apps. You can also make digital payments, deposits, and withdrwals right in the DigiCard, which saves you time and money.

Mobile Compatibility

DigiCard’s digital interface works with any mobile operating system, such as Apple and Android, so you can access their software right in the card. You also have access to use Apple's digital paying system, known as Apple Pay, to make it work hand in hand with your DigiCard. Will you look at that!
